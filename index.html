About

I am a multidisciplinary creative and aspiring front-end developer with a fine arts background, currently studying Multimedia Design at KEA in Copenhagen.

Some of the areas covered so far at KEA have been UI design and development, UX, content creation, and technology and business. In the course of my studies, I discovered a new passion in front-end development, which has only been re-affirmed by taking the front-end elective. Following this, I am now seeking an internship in this field, and dreaming of a future position where I can pursue hands-on web programming while still being able to draw upon and utilize my visual arts background. I am especially interested in developing my abilities in animating with JavaScript, and in exploring the expressive potentials of using animations to bring pages to life.

Outside of studying at KEA, I have been working professionally as a glassblower for the past 10 years in a variety of settings, from private studios to factory work, as well as teaching. As a result of these experiences, I deeply value quality craftsmanship and good teamwork, and have developed a borderline unhealthy attention to detail.
