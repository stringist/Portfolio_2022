The Party Devil Pre-game Game

Party Devil's not a bad guy; he's just looking for a good time! To pre-game is to get the party started before the party's even started; one of PD's specialties. In this rock n' roll-inspired javascript game, the player clicks on certain objects to gain points and up the festive spirit.

The game as a whole was very inspired from my own background in and around the punk and metal music scenes, and I wanted to take the opportunity to create a soundtrack that reflected that. All of the sounds effects are recorded on guitar, as well as the background music itself, which was heavily inspired by NPR, a local punk band from my hometown of Waco, TX.
